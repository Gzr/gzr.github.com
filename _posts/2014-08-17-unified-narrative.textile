A good narrative encompasses and unifies as many elements as possible. The narrative engine of a life helps it win by supplying a feeling of understanding which in turn allows for greater availability of restful and positive states. The resulting confidence expressed through body language. 

The only truth the narrative engine cares for is that we must survive.

_persistance_

You could say that the word “persisting” in ‘persisting restlessness’ is a verb and that restlessness is the thing being persisted in; because there are things to be done about restlessness. But it does have the feel of a caged in trap, or less dramatically, the asymmetrical in-out systems of the type used in pens - click in at the top, out at the side.
